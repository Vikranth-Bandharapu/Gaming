PRIVACY POLICY

How we secure telemetry scripts, encrypt agent signatures, and isolate client memory pools.

ENCRYPTION KEY: SHA-512-NODE

1. Data Caching & Telemetry Metrics

Stackly Play automatically caches real-time input telemetry, connection speeds, and frame-rate intervals to diagnose connection bottlenecks. These metrics are processed locally in volatile RAM and are automatically purged from the regional edge nodes when your terminal session is disconnected.

2. Agent Signatures & Authentication

Your account signature (email, display name, and password hashes) is secured via cryptographic salt encryptions. We do not transmit plain-text passwords across Sector 4 grids. Your secure clearance token is stored inside your browser's encrypted local storage to maintain session integrity during match changes.

3. Client-Side Memory Isolation

Our anti-cheat systems perform sandboxed integrity checks on game-execution blocks. These checks are constrained to memory allocations claimed by the Stackly launcher and do not scan adjacent user directories, network devices, or external system processes. Privacy isolation is fully maintained.

4. Third-Party Interfaces & Advertisements

We do not sell agent telemetry datasets to advertisement corporations. Stackly coordinates only with official regional hosting infrastructures and verified anti-cheat developers to enforce security operations. Social integrations (e.g. Discord, Twitch) follow the privacy matrices of their respective companies.
